About CIDAG

The Computational Intelligence and Data Analysis Group (CIDAG) is a multidisciplinary research group at the Federal University of São Paulo (Unifesp). Currently, the research group comprises 3 faculty members, 8 graduate students, 2 undergrad students, 1 postdoctoral researchers, and several collaborators. The CIDAG’s research covers several topics in Machine Learning, Data Analysis, and their applications in several problems, such as Social Network Analysis and Materials Sciences. If you are interested in joining CIDAG, see our contact section. If you are interested in Social Networks, visit the site of our Observatory at http://dmso.unifesp.br/
