André F. Oliveira

PhD candidate

LABAC/INPE

André F. Oliveira is a PhD candidate on Applied Computing in the National Institute for Space Research (INPE) in Brazil. André received his BSc. degree in physics in 2016 from the São Paulo State University (Unesp), Brazil, and his M.Sc. in Energy from the Federal University of ABC (UFBAC), Brazil, in 2019. His research interests include machine learning, complex networks, and their applications in interdisciplinary problems. In his free time, you are more likely to find him training and improving (hopefully) his swimming skills.
